**Cuber build 256** is a Developer Preview build of *[Project Cuber](Cuber)*. It was compiled on 14 March 2026 at 5:58 pm, well over a month after [the previous build](Cuber_build_113).

Tabs have also now been introduced in this build, albeit internally broken. One side effect of this is that the tab panel line does not appear. Tabs wouldn't be completely fixed until [build 351](Cuber_build_351).

Additionally, a new `whcuber_main` branch (standing for *Whistler Cuber Main*) was made, replacing the *[Panther](Panther)* decoy branches from previous builds.
