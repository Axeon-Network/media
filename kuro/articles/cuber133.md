**Cuber build 133** is a Developer Preview build of *[Project Cuber](Cuber)*. It was compiled on 11 February 2026 at 12:56 pm. This build adds the first demo of *Cuber* (named the "Change Edition Wizard") showing items like group boxes and radio buttons, depicting what would be an SKU selection wizard on beta builds of [Windows XP](https://betawiki.net/wiki/Windows_XP).
