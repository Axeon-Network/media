***Project Cuber, Minimal Edition***, commonly shortened to ***Minimal Cuber*** and ***MinCuber***, is an upcoming website created by [KitSixtyFour](KitSixtyFour) intended to be a replacement to *[HoriWebsite](HoriWebsite)* and its cancelled successor tentatives. Even though it serves the same purpose as the original *[Project Cuber](Cuber)*, it can be considered a "Demo Version" of such, since it builds upon the codebase of the Alpha Release milestone, and includes various personal site elements, such as a blog.
