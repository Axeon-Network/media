**StuffyXP Hangout** was a Discord server created by [Kit](KitSixtyFour). It was created on 3 October 2024 to replace Kit's previous and first server [Stuffy's Place](Stuffy's_Place) for a "fresh start".

As opposed to the previous server, STXP Hangout was built to be focused around the YouTube channel [StuffyXP](StuffyXP) rather than Kit themselves, idea inspired from [LynxPlaza](Axeon_Network#Discord_Server) which after [Avery](AveryEclipse)'s personal online identity rebrand from Lynxmic to Clyron, became a channel-focused server.

The server hit 25 members by 16 October 2024, overtaking Stuffy's Place's member count, and eventually came to be one of the main, active servers of its community bubble between November 2024 and January 2025, the server's peak.

The server however went on a downfall after January 2025. Kit handed over StuffyXP Hangout's (and Stuffy's Place's) ownership to Avery and "quit" Discord in favor of Stoat (formerly Revolt) on 3 February 2025, where they created [HoriServer (1)](HoriServer).

Kit returned on 1 March 2025 "for 1 day or 2", which however later became to be a comeback "for good". The server picked up activity but was later shut down on 16 March 2025 alongside the StuffyXP channels, and was replaced by [HoriServer (2)](HoriServer_(2)).
