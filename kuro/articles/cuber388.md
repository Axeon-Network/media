**Cuber build 388** is an Alpha Escrow build of *[Project Cuber](Cuber)*. It was compiled on 2 May 2026 at 2:24 pm. It is the first build to be compiled under the "Alpha Release Escrow" umbrella and the first build compiled from the `wh-cuber_alpha_escrow` branch. 

This build introduces the text box items, including the text area. Additionally, the watermark can now report itself as an "Evaluation copy" instead of being "For testing purposes only", in preparation of the public Alpha Release.
