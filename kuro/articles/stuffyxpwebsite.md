The ***StuffyXP Website*** was [KitSixtyFour](KitSixtyFour)'s personal website from September to December 2024. It was created as the successor to *[Stuffy's Website](Stuffy's_Website_(June_2024))*, which used the older [Dogui](Dogui)-era branding. It was replaced by *[HoriWebsite](HoriWebsite)* in January 2025 following Kit's personal alias rebrand from StuffyXP to Horibyte.

Development of the *StuffyXP Website* began in August 2024 to find a replacement to *Stuffy's Website*, which became visually outdated following the introduction of the StuffyXP branding one month earlier. By late September, the website was considered finished with the compilation of [build 1986](StuffyXP_Website_build_1986), which was published to `stuffyxp.github.io` not long after, effectively replacing the older site which could still be viewed on the `stuffywebsitewou` subdirectory. Throughout its lifespan, the website got updates, further improving the site. Its last update, [Update 2.1.2](StuffyXP_Website_build_2100), was released in February 2025.

Many features and overall structured were ported and/or rewritten to the website, such as the blog or social boxes. Similar to its predecessor, *StuffyXP Website* is built with the Bulma CSS framework and Jekyll site generator, being hosted on GitHub Pages. A *Downloads* page was introduced with [Update 2.1](StuffyXP_Website_build_2091), reaching a similar goal to the planned "Software Repository" meant to be part of the [original *Stuffy's Website*](Stuffy's_Website_(February_2024)). While not as massive as the latter was planned to be, things such as a Demo Version of Nintendo's 2009 Nintendo DS RPG *[Mario & Luigi: Bowser's Inside Story](https://mariowiki.com/wiki/Mario_&_Luigi:_Bowser's_Inside_Story)* were hosted on it.

The site was succedded by the *[Horibyte Website](HoriWebsite)* (now *HoriWebsite*) in January 2025 following the StuffyXP-Horibyte identity split, where Kit's personal alias was re-branded from StuffyXP to Horibyte, splitting the channel and the person behind it. Although *HoriWebsite* was written from scratch, many features were ported to the site, like the Downloads page, social boxes and even the blog index.

The site's repository was archived on 3 February 2025, while the website can still be viewed to this day at [snowybifox.github.io/stuffyxp-website](https://snowybifox.github.io/stuffyxp-website).
