**Cuber build 100** is a Developer Preview build of *[Project Cuber](Cuber)*. It was compiled on 10 February 2026 at 7:42 pm from the decoy 26-02-10_kit branch. It introduces all five types of window controls (that is, close, maximize, restore (although it is not used), minimize and help), alongside inactive and parent-type windows.
