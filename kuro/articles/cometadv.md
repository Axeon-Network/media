***Comet Adventure*** (codenamed *Project Retriever* or just *Retriever*) is a planned role-playing game for PCs and console platforms previously developed and created by [KitSixtyFour](KitSixtyFour). Various release dates for the game were planned; the most recent stating a public demo release (albeit using the most stable beta version of the game) releasing on **10 May 2026**, and the finished version releasing on **27 January 2027**[^1].

While the [development of Comet Adventure](Development_of_Comet Adventure) began in late 2025 (under the *StarCore v1* moniker), various [concepts and ideas](Conception_of_Comet_Adventure) for *Project Retriever* began in early 2016, on which at said point, the game was drastically different from the finished product: ideas such as [Dogui](Dogui) being the main protagonist or a completely different story are distinct features of it.

# Plot
The game's main plot details [Comet](Comet) (a Cocker Spaniel dog, often confused for a Golden Retriever) and [Daylight](Daylight) (also a Cocker Spaniel)'s quest on [NovaLand](NovaLand#comet-adventure) to stop the game's antagonist, [Kydro](Kydro) (a nine-tailed kitsune) from taking over NovaLand.
