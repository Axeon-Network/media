**KitSixtyFour**, alternatively known as **StupidBiFox**, and more commonly reffered to as **Kit** (born 19 June, 2010) is a Honduran web developer, furry artist, tech hobbyist, content creator and occasional gamer.

They are primarily known for creating content for the [StuffyXP](StuffyXP) YouTube channel and more recently [AstroNT](AstroNT) alongside [Avery](AveryEclipse), creating and developing the *[Axeon Akane](Akane)* engine (and its commonplace implementation *[KuroWiki](KuroWiki)*), the *[Horicraft](Horicraft)* Minecraft server (and its successor *[Horicraft (2)](Horicraft_(2))*), alongside many [other works](List_of_KitSixtyFour's_projects).

# Personal life
Kit was born in Honduras on 19 June, 2010 and is the youngest of a family of three sibilings. Although they haven't talked much of their family, it is known that their dad had influenced the introduction of [NishiLappy](NishiLappy), and their uncle had given [TheCeleryPC](TheCeleryPC) to them.

Kit goes by they/them pronouns and came out as demiboy and bisexual (which is their current status, since it has changed within the span of 2 years) and their timezone is the Central Standard Time (or CST), a.k.a UTC-6.[^1] Since June 2026, Kit is on known to be on a relationship.

They have also stated that they are a perfectionist and have ADHD, which causes them to either scrap entire projects due to minor details (the more notable example being *[Project Retriever](Comet_Adventure)*), or not work in existing ones at all since they "have a thought then just forget about it 15 seconds later". Kit is also known to have sleep problems, which as they've described, "it slowly kills me [them]". Kit has also stated that since 2024, their mental status "is not good at all", which are major causes of the previous Discord breaks.

Since January 2024, Kit began friendship with [Avery](AveryEclipse). One of the most notable projects by them are KuroWiki, for which they develop together, [AstroNT](AstroNT), and initially the [Avery and Kit Collab](Avery_and_Kit_Collab), which is the first project they've done together.
