**AstroNT Embedded**, commonly shortened to **AstroNTe**, is a YouTube channel ran and owned by [KitSixtyFour](KitSixtyFour) and [AveryEclipse](AveryEclipse) created and launcher on 6 June 2026. It is the sidekick channel to [AstroNT Workstation](AstroNT), serving as "an extras channel".

The channel was created as a response to the amount of unused AstroNT material that wouldn't qualify as "a main channel release". It is named after [Windows NT Embedded 4.0](https://betawiki.net/wiki/Windows_NT_Embedded_4.0), following the gimmick of "a smaller channel [to dump stuff on]". The channel's branding follows a gray-scale version of the original AstroNT branding, alongside the "Embedded" subtitle.
