**KuroWiki build 3567 (main_wik)** is the very first build of [KuroWiki](KuroWiki) (then-known as HoriWiki), compiled on 4 May 2025 at 10:45 UTC-6. This build is a fork of [HoriWebsite](HoriWebsite) v2.4 build 3567.

There are virtually no differences between the actual HoriWebsite v2.4 and this build of KuroWiki, albeit from the fact it was compiled from a new `main-wik` development branch. This fork came to existance due to a issue with Jekyll's page generation system not working when [Kit](KitSixtyFour) used the `jekyll new` command.

Another compile of this build, namely `2.4.3567.horiwiki-pa1.250504-1052`, was compiled 7 minutes later, where "pa1" means Pre-Alpha 1.
