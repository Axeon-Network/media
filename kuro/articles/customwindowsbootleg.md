***Making a Custom Windows Bootleg***[^1] is a YouTube video made by [KitSixtyFour](KitSixtyFour) for the [AstroNT](AstroNT) channel, becoming the 10th video overall.

The video focuses on the making process of a bootleg known as "*[Microsoft Windows Nishi & LeCat Edition 2026 Version 2003 R2 Home Premium](Windows_Nishi_&_LeCat_Edition_2026_Version_2003_R2_Home_Premium)*" based on Windows Vista Service Pack 2.

# Notes
[^1]: Alternatively known as ***Making a Custom Windows Bootleg (but Everything Goes Wrong)*** and ***How to (NOT) make a Windows Bootleg*** during YouTube's A/B testing period. Commonly shortened to ***MaCWB*** or ***Custom Windows Bootleg***.
