**Comet** (cannonically **Comet Nishi Adsurden**[^1] [^2]) is a fictional character created by [Kit](Kit64). It was also Kit's fursona from when it was introduced up until November 2025, and is the main protagonist of the up-coming [Comet Adventure](Comet_Adventure) video game series.

# Physical appearance

While in his dog form, Comet has light yellow fur with light brown spots in his head, some parts of his back and his tail.

While in his fox form, Comet has the same light yellow full all over his body, with brown marks around his nose, completely his ears and the tip of his tail.
