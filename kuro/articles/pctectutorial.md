**pc tec tutorial** is an inside joke meme in the [AveryEclipse](AveryEclipse)/[Lynxmic](Lynxmic) universe, from late 2024-early 2025.

The original meaning dates back to a Lynxmic sponsorship email from sometime around October-November 2024, shown on the [LynxPlaza](Axeon_Network) Discord server.

Members of LynxPlaza including Avery themselves have pointed out this specific phrase in the original screenshot, highlighting the not-so-best grammar which became to be an inside joke for the next several months in the community:

> ... which specializes in software of **pc tec tutorial**.

Although the Lynxmic contact email used to be included in the channel about page, it was only meant to be used as a contact method for the general channel audience. Avery had never accepted any sponsorship offers on the Lynxmic YouTube channel, which continued to appear in the channel's contact email inbox even after the email was removed from the about.
