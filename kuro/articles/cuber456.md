**Cuber build 456** is an Alpha Escrow build of *[Project Cuber](Cuber)*. It was compiled on 2 May 2026 at 8:45 pm, exactly 45 minutes after [the previous build](Cuber_build_418).

This build features an almost-complete implementation of the Demo page, that is, the tab demo windows (both for single and dual rows) are completed and positioned correctly. Additionally, the "Text Boxes" group was added to the Button Controls window, where alluding to its name, showcases various types of text boxes, including the text area.
