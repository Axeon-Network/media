**Cuber build 1** is the first build of *[Project Cuber](Cuber)*, a successor to *[HoriWebsite](HoriWebsite)* and its cancelled successors. It was compiled on 9 February 2026 at 8:52 pm from the `26-02-09_kit` branch, which is a decoy branch used by *[Panther](Panther)* when a Git branch could not be found.

Not much can be said about this build aside that it identifies itself as a "Developer Preview" and is very barebones. It does not feature any of the Watercolor elements and just has a line reading "sneaky..." above the watermark.
