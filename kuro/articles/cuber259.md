**Cuber build 259** is a Developer Preview build of *[Project Cuber](Cuber)*. It was compiled on 15 March 2026 at 6:34 pm. This build does not actually bring any new features, as it breaks the tab items instead. More specifically, tabs are not correctly attached to the tab panel, and the left corner of first tab of an inactive row has a separator on it. However, the titles of each tab have been changed to the version family and number from [Windows 95](https://betawiki.net/wiki/Windows_95) to [Windows Server 2003](https://betawiki.net/wiki/Windows_Server_2003), with their contents being their respective codenames (i.e. "Microsoft Windows NT 5.1" having "Microsoft Windows Whistler").
