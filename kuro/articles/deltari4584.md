**Deltari build 4584** is a Release Candidate 1 build of Deltari, compiled on 13 January 2026 at 11:46 UTC-6 from the `main` branch for [Deltari Meta](Deltari_Meta).

Screenshots of this build have been posted on the [Axeon Network](Axeon_Network) Discord server, showcasing the homepage and drawer. Unlike [build 4500](Deltari_build_4500), it includes RC1 works ported from the [KuroWiki](KuroWiki) `kitsixtyfour` development branch absent in the former build.
